How Publishing has Impacted Me

The closest thing I have to publishing is entering projects into the Ozaukee County Fair, and that is a big part of me. It allows me to share what I see with others. It's always entertaining seeing people pausing to look at my work, sometimes praising how beautiful the photos look. It's even more rewarding when family and friends see my work and compliment on how well I did.
